- About me -

I build companies and products

Ever since I was a little kid I have loved building things. What started as Lego eventually transformed into code, and by the time I was 12 I was hacking on opensource and installing Linux on stuff. I finally got to Skidmore College and dove into a CS education with both feet. I knew for sure I wanted to build software for a living when I stayed up for 3 days straight to build an emulator and assembler for a custom ISA I helped design.

But I rapidly realized that I didn't just want to build things. By the time I graduated from school I had an itch for entrepreneurship. So without really knowing what I was getting myself into, I started my first startup journey. I was lucky enough to have an awesome array for mentors and friends who helped me along the way.

Now days I help others get their companies and ideas off of the ground, in addition to working on my own. Along the way I accidentaly built a network of cool people in both startups and engineering, as well as building up expertise in a wide variety of spaces. My primary focuses are around data visualization, fintech, and SaaS companies, but I have been known to branch out as well.

I would love to help you turn your concept into a living breathing company!
