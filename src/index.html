- About me -

I build companies and products

Ever since I was a little kid I have loved building things. What started as Lego eventually transformed into code, and by the time I was 12 I was hacking on open source and installing Linux on stuff. I got to Skidmore College and dove into a CS education with both feet. I knew I wanted to build software for a living when I stayed up for 3 days straight to build an emulator and assembler for a custom ISA I helped design.

But I rapidly realized that I didn't just want to build things. By the time I graduated from school I had an itch for entrepreneurship. So without really knowing what I was getting myself into, I began my first startup journey. I was lucky enough to have a remarkable array of mentors and friends who helped me along the way.

Now a days I help others get their companies and ideas off the ground, in addition to working on my own company. Along the way, I had the good fortune to build a network of cool people in both startups and engineering, as well as building up expertise in a wide variety of spaces. My primary focuses are around data visualization, machine learning, and SaaS companies.

I would love to help you turn your concept into a living, breathing company!

I joined Kinetic in 2017 after their acquisition of Windrush. At Kinetic I lead the software engineering team, and am responsible for hiring and management as well as cracking tough technical challenges that arise from ingesting and running machine learning algorithims on billions of data points a day.

Windrush - Founder and CEO / CTO


2013 - 2017 (Aquired)


I founded Windrush in 2013 with the goal of building the next generation of data visualization products. I assembled a skilled and dedicated team and grew the company to 8 people. I built the platform from a blank slate into 150k LoC product deployed at scale, and raised a pre-seed and seed round of financing from both institutional and angel investors. I was responsible for day-to-day operations as well as strategy. I helped sell and deploy to a wide array of customers, including some of the largest financial institutions in the world. Ultimately I guided to company to a sale to Kinetic in 2017.
